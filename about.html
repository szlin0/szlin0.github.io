Our Story

Meet George and Amy!

Lots of Dogs Cafe is owned and operated by husband-and-wife duo George and Amy. Having a dog cafe has been a longtime dream of theirs, and they are so happy to finally bring it to life in the beautiful town of Chapel Hill!

Frequently Asked Questions

Are the dogs at Lots of Dogs Cafe up for adoption?
Yes, all of the dogs at our cafe are adoptable and eagerly awaiting their forever home. If you are interested in adopting one of the dogs at the cafe, please fill out the Adoption Interest Form.
Am I allowed to bring my own dog to Lots of Dogs Cafe?
No! For the health and safety of our patrons and adoptable dogs, we do not allow outside dogs to come into the cafe.
Do I need a reservation to visit Lots of Dogs Cafe?
Nope, we operate on a walk-in basis. Come visit us anytime from 8 am-5 pm Monday through Friday!
